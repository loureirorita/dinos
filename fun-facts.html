Dinosaur Fun Facts

Dinosaur Eggs

Some dinosaurs laid eggs that were the size of a soccer ball, while others laid eggs as small as a marble!

Fossilized Bones

Most dinosaur fossils are found in sedimentary rocks, which form from layers of mud and sand.

Flying Dinosaurs

Not all dinosaurs were ground-dwellers; some, like Pteranodon, were excellent fliers with wingspans up to 7 meters!

Giant Dinosaurs

The largest dinosaurs, like Argentinosaurus, could weigh up to 100 tons and reach lengths of over 30 meters!

Speedy Dinosaurs

Some dinosaurs, such as the Velociraptor, could run up to 64 km/h, making them incredibly fast hunters.

Smart Dinosaurs

Some dinosaurs, like the Troodon, had larger brains relative to their body size, suggesting they were quite intelligent.
